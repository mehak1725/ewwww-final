About Us

At EWWWW – Eco Way to a Waste-Free World Wide Web, we're turning everyday actions into eco-friendly impact. Our platform blends AI and sustainability to help you make better choices—starting with the waste in your hand. Snap a photo of your trash, and our AI will help identify what it is and how to dispose of it properly. Need ideas for upcycling or tips to live more sustainably? We've got suggestions. Want to find a recycling bin nearby? We'll point the way. We believe tech should work for the planet, not against it. With EWWWW, you're not just scanning waste—you're creating a cleaner future, one smart step at a time.
