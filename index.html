About Us

At EWWWW – Eco Way to a Waste-free World Wide Web, we’re turning everyday actions into eco-friendly impact. Our platform scans, classifies and sustainably helps you make better choices – starting with the waste in your hand. Unsure if that old charger is e-waste or not? Not sure what to do with your banana peel or printer? We help sort it properly. Need ideas for upcycling or tips on how to reuse sustainably? We’ve got suggestions. Want to find recycling bins nearby? We’ll guide the way. We believe tech should work for the planet, not against it. With EWWWW, you're not just scanning waste—you’re creating a cleaner future, one smart step at a time.
